# Playbooks

Operational SOPs for the UK + Hong Kong AI Luxury Resale OS.

- `daily-sop.md`: founder daily workflow.
- `proof-pack-sop.md`: evidence requirements before listing and dispatch.
- `uk-market-sop.md`: UK listing, delivery, and dispute workflow.
- `hk-market-sop.md`: Hong Kong listing, FPS/PayMe, SF Express, and face-to-face workflow.
- `sourcing-sop.md`: buy/watch/reject rules before buying stock.
- `fulfilment-sop.md`: payment, packing, tracking, SF Express, and face-to-face handover rules.
- `live-commerce-sop.md`: AI live selling and content workflow.
- `customer-support-sop.md`: safe AI support and escalation.
- `risk-legal-sop.md`: anti-counterfeit, fake-claim, chargeback, and platform safety.
- `video-factory-sop.md`: product videos, AI host scripts, live cutdowns, and daily video calendar.
- `acquisition-sop.md`: ads, old-buyer reactivation, Discord VIP, Snapchat growth, and consent rules.
- `platform-connectors-sop.md`: Shopify/TikTok main-platform connector rules, mock mode, event tracking, and proof gates.
