# Risk and Legal SOP

## Prohibited

- Fake goods.
- Replica goods.
- Logo-copy goods.
- 1:1, mirror quality, AAA grade wording.
- Fake reviews, fake buyers, fake sales, or fake scarcity.
- Official authorised reseller claims without documentation.

## Listing wording

- Luxury resale: pre-owned, condition shown, proof available where applicable.
- Budget fashion: budget fashion, inspired style, Korean style, quiet luxury look.
- Budget fashion must not pretend to be designer.

## Evidence-first rule

All disputes are handled from evidence:

- Product proof pack.
- Customer messages.
- Payment record.
- Packing and tracking.
- Return condition and serial/code match.

Legal templates are operational drafts and require UK/HK professional review.
