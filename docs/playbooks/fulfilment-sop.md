# Fulfilment SOP

## UK

- Confirm platform payment.
- Match SKU and proof pack.
- Film item details and packing.
- Save parcel weight.
- Use tracked Royal Mail / Evri / DPD.
- Send tracking to buyer.

## Hong Kong

- Confirm FPS / PayMe / bank transfer / platform payment.
- Save payment screenshot and transaction reference.
- For SF Express: save waybill and tracking.
- For face-to-face: meet in public location and ask buyer to confirm receipt in message.

## Risk

Never ship high-risk or high-value orders without payment proof and packing evidence.
