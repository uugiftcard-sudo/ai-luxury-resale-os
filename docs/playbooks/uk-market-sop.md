## Disputes

If a buyer claims an item is fake, pause AI replies and collect:

- Product proof pack.
- Listing text and photos.
- Buyer photos.
- Platform case.
- Packing video.
- Parcel weight.
- Tracking.
- Return-item matching photos.

Never remove statutory rights through return wording.
