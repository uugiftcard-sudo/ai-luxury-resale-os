# Hong Kong Market SOP

## Platforms

- Carousell HK
- Instagram
- Facebook Marketplace
- Shopify HK
- TikTok/Shorts
- WhatsApp/Telegram VIP group

## Payments

- FPS
- PayMe for Business
- Bank transfer
- Stripe/Shopify Payments where suitable

## Logistics

- SF Express
- SF lockers
- Local courier
- Face-to-face trade in public/CCTV-friendly locations

## Face-to-face trade

- Confirm SKU, price, meeting point, and included accessories in chat.
- Take product condition photos before meeting.
- Ask buyer to confirm receipt in message after handover.
- Keep payment screenshot/reference.

## Counterfeit risk

Carousell prohibits counterfeit items and Hong Kong Customs can enforce against online counterfeit sales. Do not sell fake, replica, 1:1, or misleading logo-copy items.
