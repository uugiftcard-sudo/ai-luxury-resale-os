# Product Proof Pack SOP

Every luxury resale item needs a proof pack before serious selling.

## Required evidence

- Source record and seller conversation.
- Receipt or invoice where available.
- Serial/code/label photos where applicable.
- Front, back, inside, logo, stitching, hardware, and flaw photos.
- Condition grade and honest condition notes.
- Packing video before dispatch.
- Parcel weight and tracking for posted orders.
- Payment proof for HK FPS/PayMe/bank transfer.
- Face-to-face confirmation record for HK meetups.

## Rule

The system can say evidence is available. It must not make unsupported authenticity guarantees or claim authorised reseller status.
